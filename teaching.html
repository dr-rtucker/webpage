Crime Analysis Through Python Learning Material

I have developed a curriculum that teaches statistics to undergraduate students through Python. The philosphy of this curriculum is to minimize the most frustrating parts of coding (installations, servers, terminals, etc.) while teaching students how to answer social science research questions using statistics and real data.

All tutorials have been published below using the browser-based Google Colab platform. I have also included links to download data used in all exercises - I encourage you to follow along!
